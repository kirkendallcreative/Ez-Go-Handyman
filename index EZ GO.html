About Shem

Meet Shem Prattis

Shem Prattis began his career in plumbing, building hands-on experience across multiple companies over four years. In 2015, he moved to Hamilton to pursue his passion for real estate, successfully completing his first home flip and later renovating a triplex.

He went on to work as a superintendent, managing a 245-unit residential portfolio, and gained further experience with several property management companies. In 2022, recognizing a need for greater professionalism in the industry, Shem founded Ez Go Handyman Services to deliver reliable, high-quality service to the community.
